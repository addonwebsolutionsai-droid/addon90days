# AddonWeb Parent Brand Rebrand

**This is the rebrand for the AddonWebSolutions parent company — the umbrella over all 7 products.** Each individual product has its own brand direction in its PRD.

---

## The rebrand mission (one paragraph)

Transform AddonWebSolutions from a "custom web and mobile app development" shop into an **AI-native product studio with deep IoT × AI expertise**. The website, marketing, content, and social should make a first-time visitor think: "These are the engineers running an AI-first company themselves — of course I want them building for me."

## New positioning (parent brand)

**One-liner:** AI-native product studio. IoT × AI specialists.

**Full positioning:**

For companies building AI-powered products or deploying IoT × AI solutions, **AddonWebSolutions is an engineering studio that runs as a 13-agent AI-first company itself** and applies that rare real-world expertise — in Claude agents, IoT hardware, firmware, and production AI systems — to build products and enterprise solutions. Unlike pure-software AI consultancies who've never shipped hardware, or traditional IoT shops who've never integrated AI agents, we bring both sides.

**Short form for site hero:**
> AI-native product studio. 10 years of hardware. 13 Claude agents. Production-grade IoT × AI solutions.

## Rebrand phasing

Don't try to redo everything on Day 1. Phase it into the 90-day runbook.

### Phase 1 — Foundation (Days 25-30, per runbook)
- New positioning agreed
- New website drafted, deployed to preview
- New visual direction committed
- Brand voice documented (done — see `operations/brand-voice.md`)
- Day 26: new website LIVE

### Phase 2 — Content engine alive (Days 31-60)
- Content output at 20-30 pieces/week
- Social media automation running
- First case studies from our own products
- Email newsletter launched
- YouTube channel with 4+ videos

### Phase 3 — Compounding (Days 61-90)
- Day 89: publish the flagship "How we ran a 13-agent AI company for 90 days" case study
- All rebrand elements integrated
- Social accounts at 5k-10k+ followers each
- Monthly newsletter with 1k+ subscribers

## Where agents pull inspiration

Before any rebrand work, agents MUST read:
- `DESIGN_STANDARDS.md` — quality bar
- `operations/brand-voice.md` — voice + banned phrases
- `inspiration/` — founder's manual-fill inspiration library

If the founder hasn't filled in `inspiration/` yet, the rebrand work pauses. Agents prompt the founder to add inspiration before proceeding — without it, designs default to generic best-practice instead of matching taste.
