# ChatBase Launch — LinkedIn Post

**Post:**

We just launched ChatBase — a WhatsApp AI agent built specifically for Indian small businesses.

The insight is simple: India's SMB layer runs on WhatsApp, not email or CRM. A jeweller, a cloud kitchen, a wholesale distributor — they all have one WhatsApp Business number and one owner drowning in 100+ messages a day.

ChatBase sits on that number and handles the repetitive load:

- Price queries answered from your product catalog
- Order acknowledgements with missing-detail follow-up
- Invoice requests flagged and acknowledged
- Payment status queries resolved from your KB
- Complaints escalated to you — always, no exceptions
- Unclear messages answered with a clarifying question

The AI doesn't guess. It doesn't hallucinate prices. It answers from what you teach it. When it's not confident enough, it escalates to you rather than making something up.

During beta, it's free. No monthly fee. No credit card. You connect your WhatsApp Business number (requires Meta Cloud API verification) and paste your business content into the knowledge base.

We're an Ahmedabad-based engineering firm. 10 years of IoT and enterprise software. This is our pivot to AI-native products — ChatBase is product #2.

Demo at: https://addon90days.vercel.app/dashboard/chatbase (MockChat widget works without a Meta number)

If you run or advise a WhatsApp-heavy business and want to try it — reach out directly.
