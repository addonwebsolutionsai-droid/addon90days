---

**Tweet 2 (the problem)**
Every Indian SMB owner on WhatsApp is doing this manually:

- Replying to "what is price of X?" 40 times a day
- Sending the same payment UPI QR to every customer
- Waking up to 60 unread messages at 7 AM

That's 2–3 hours of repetitive replies. Every. Single. Day.

---

**Tweet 3 (the product)**
ChatBase is an AI agent that sits on your WhatsApp Business number.

You feed it your KB:
→ Paste your product list, prices, business hours, FAQs

It classifies every incoming message into one of 6 intents:
→ Price inquiry, order, invoice, payment status, complaint, unknown

Then it replies — in plain Hindi-English — from your content.

---

**Tweet 4 (the key differentiator)**
Complaints are auto-escalated to you. No AI tries to handle "my order arrived broken."

Low-confidence messages are escalated too — the AI knows when it doesn't know.

You get a clean inbox of only the conversations that need a human.

Everything ships inside one Vercel deploy. Zero new infra.
