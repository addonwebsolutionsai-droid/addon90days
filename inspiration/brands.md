# Brand Inspiration

**Visual and verbal brand identities you admire.** Covers logos, color palettes, typography choices, overall brand personality.

Used during parent brand rebrand + when defining each product's sub-brand.

---

## Format suggestion

For each brand, include:
- **Name / URL**
- **What I admire:** (logo, color, type, voice, personality, all of the above)
- **Vibe in one sentence** (how the brand feels to you)
- **Specific assets to study** (their logo treatment, their brand book if public, their merch, etc.)
- **Use for** (parent brand? specific product? just inspiration for voice?)
